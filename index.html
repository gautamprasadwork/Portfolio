Volatility Spillover & Market Connectedness — NIFTY50, S&P500, VIX, WTI Oil (2013–2025)

GitHub ↗
NIFTY50 is a net receiver of global shocks. VIX and S&P500 are the dominant transmitters. Total Connectedness Index (TCI) spikes sharply during all three crisis episodes — 2015–16 (commodity crash), COVID-2020, and 2022 (Ukraine/Fed tightening) — confirming cross-market integration intensifies under stress.

Built a VAR model on 12 years of daily log-returns with lag selection via Schwarz Criterion. Applied the static Diebold-Yilmaz (2012) spillover index using 20-step-ahead FEVD to compute directional TO/FROM/NET connectedness. Followed with a 180-day rolling window to produce a time-varying Total Connectedness Index (TCI). Pairwise spillover series (SPX↔NIFTY, VIX↔NIFTY, OIL↔NIFTY) then quantified channel-specific transmission. Crisis-period averages isolate how each channel activates differently per episode.

WACC Estimation — ORLEN Group S.A. (PKN, WSE) · FY2024

GitHub ↗
WACC = 8.8% · βL = 0.92 · Ke = 11.07% · Kd (after-tax) = 4.40% · Capital structure: 66% equity / 34% debt (total firm value PLN 86.8bn)

Selected ORLEN Group (PKN ORLEN), one of Central Europe's largest integrated energy companies (WIG30). Estimated levered beta using the Hamada equation — unlevered β = 0.65 from Damodaran's energy sector data, re-levered at ORLEN's 52% D/E ratio and 19% Polish CIT to yield βL = 0.92. Applied CAPM (Rf = 6%, MRP = 5.5%) to derive Ke = 11.07%. Cost of debt estimated from FY2024 consolidated financials: PLN 1.605bn interest expense / PLN 29.54bn total interest-bearing liabilities = 5.43% pre-tax, 4.40% after-tax.

DCF Firm Valuation — ORLEN Group S.A. (PKN) · Forecast 2025–2029

GitHub ↗
Intrinsic value: 104.95 PLN vs market price: 107.96 PLN → Stock slightly overvalued by 2.8%. Total firm value: PLN 151.3bn (PV of FCFFs: 38.2bn + PV of terminal value: 102.0bn + non-operating assets: 11.0bn).

Built a full FCFF model (EBIT(1−T) + D&A − ΔWorking Capital − Net CapEx) over a 5-year explicit horizon, anchored to 18.3% sales growth (2020–2024 historical avg) and 10.92% EBIT margin. Discounted at WACC = 8.8% from Assignment 1. Residual value computed via Gordon Growth Model with g = 0% (conservative energy sector assumption), yielding PV(RV) = PLN 102bn. Equity value derived by subtracting net debt from enterprise value and dividing by 1.16bn shares outstanding.

Financial Statement Analysis — Lockheed Martin (NYSE: LMT) · FY2022–2024

GitHub ↗
ROE of 81% is misleading — DuPont decomposition reveals it is driven by extreme financial leverage (8.21x equity multiplier), not earnings quality. Net margin declined from 10.2% to 7.5% while D/E surged from 1.70x to 3.20x. Recommendation: Hold. Reduce leverage to below D/E 2.0x within 3 years.

Applied the 6-phase CFA Financial Analysis Framework to Lockheed Martin ($71.1B revenue, ~$120B market cap), world's largest defense contractor, using SEC 10-K filings (US GAAP) and Bloomberg data. Revenue grew 7.7% ($66B→$71.1B) but gross profit fell $1.3B and net income dropped 23% ($6.9B→$5.3B) — gross margin compressed 300bps due to F-35 Block 4 cost overruns and supply chain inflation. DuPont ROE = 7.5% × 1.31x × 8.21x. OCF/Net Income = 1.31x (strong earnings quality). CCC = 19.6 days. Peer-benchmarked against RTX, NOC, and GD — LMT leads on ROA (9.6% vs 3.0–8.5%) but carries the highest leverage (D/E 3.20x vs peer avg 0.85x). Delivered as a 20-slide presentation.

AgenticTrading — AI-Driven Indian Stock Market Trading System

GitHub ↗
Multi-agent LLM system for live NSE trading with deterministic risk control. Supports two parallel workflows: intraday BUY/SELL equity positions and short straddle lifecycle management (NIFTY/BANKNIFTY options). Paper trading by default; capital: ₹5,00,000. @RiskGuard acts as an unbypassable final gate before every execution.

Built on Django 5.1 + LangGraph with Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6) as the trading brain. The system runs a named AI virtual team — @DirectionalTrader plans equity trades from intraday OHLCV structure, @OptionsStrategist manages straddle positions (HOLD/CLOSE/HEDGE/ROLL), @DataAnalyst pulls live market data from Angel One SmartAPI, and @RiskGuard validates 9 deterministic criteria before any order is placed. Every decision — including rejected trades — is journaled to AuditLog. RAG retrieval via @PortfolioTracker provides capital and P&L context to the LLM planner at runtime.
